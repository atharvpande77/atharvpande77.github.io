Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Who verifies citizen journalist accounts?

Newspaper admins verify accounts. Verification can include email confirmation, phone OTP and optional ID upload before granting the "Verified Reporter" role.

Can AI publish stories automatically?

No. AI assists with drafting. Every submission must go through the editor review workflow and be explicitly approved before publishing.

How many citizen journalists can I add?

Plan limits determine allowed active citizen journalists. Starter supports 10, Professional 50, and Enterprise offers unlimited contributors or custom limits.

Which CMS platforms do you support?

Out of the box: WordPress & Drupal. News Rounds also supports JSON/RSS/webhooks for integration with any DMS or custom CMS.

How is contributor data stored and protected?

We store data on secure, audited cloud services with encryption at rest and in transit. ID uploads are stored with restricted access and retention policies.

Does News Rounds support multiple languages?

Yes. The AI Reporting Agent supports drafting and translation in multiple languages; we can localize prompts and templates for your newsroom.

What if we need custom workflows or SSO?

Enterprise plans include SSO, custom workflows, dedicated onboarding and SLA-backed support. Contact sales to discuss requirements.
