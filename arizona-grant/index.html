Common Questions

What Arizona newsrooms
ask us most.

Straight answers, no sales speak.

Q Do we have to pay anything if we don’t get the grant?

No. Writing your tech section is completely free, whether you’re selected or not. We invest the time because we know our platform well enough to describe it precisely — and a well-written application helps everyone.

Q Are you affiliated with ALNF? Can you guarantee selection?

We are not affiliated with ALNF. We are an independent technology company. ALNF makes all selection decisions independently. We cannot guarantee, predict, or influence any outcome — we improve your application’s quality, not its result.

Q What if ALNF awards us less than the full platform cost?

The deployment scope is designed to fit within the ALNF maximum award of $10,000. On our free call, we’ll walk through exactly what’s included at different award levels so there are no surprises.

Q We’re a small team. Can we actually run this?

Yes — that’s what it’s built for. The editorial dashboard routes stories automatically. The citizen journalist app handles contributor intake. Training and onboarding are included in the deployment.

Q What happens after the 12-month grant period ends?

We’ll discuss continuation options well before the year ends. There’s no cliff, no surprise contracts. Your newsroom retains full control of its platform and content regardless of what you decide.

Yes — that’s what the free call is for. Or visit sm.tarunbharat.net right now to see it running in production.
