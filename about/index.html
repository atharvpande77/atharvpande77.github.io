Our Story

Built to solve a problem
we kept seeing everywhere.

PressGenAI started with a simple observation: communities everywhere have thousands of untold stories, and the newsrooms that should be telling them are running out of capacity to tell them.

Why we built this

Every regional newspaper, every digital news startup, every hyperlocal publisher faces the same constraint: covering more communities, more stories, more angles ‚Äî with a team that can't grow as fast as the need. The problem isn't a shortage of stories. It's a shortage of infrastructure to capture, edit, and publish them.

PressGenAI is that infrastructure. Not an AI that replaces journalism, but a platform that gives communities a structured, editorially controlled channel to contribute to the newsrooms that serve them ‚Äî and gives editors the tools to manage it without chaos.

What we do

We deploy white-labelled hyperlocal news platforms for newspapers, digital publishers, and newsrooms of any size. Your platform lives on your domain, carries your brand, and runs entirely under your editorial control. Community contributors submit stories through a structured portal. AI helps them draft. Your editors review, refine, and approve before anything publishes.

The result: more local stories, more community voices, and a sustainable journalism infrastructure ‚Äî without proportionally growing your team or your costs.

Who we work with

PressGenAI is designed for publishers who want to grow their coverage without growing their overhead. Our current and target customers include:
