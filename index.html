How It Works

Intelligent. Effortless. Trusted.

From story idea to published news — in minutes.

STEP 01 ✏️
Creator Spots a Story

Citizen journalists log in, pick a city, and either write manually or let AI generate a story from a basic idea.

STEP 02 🤖
AI Writes in Your Style

Answer a few guided questions. PressGenAI generates a complete, publish-ready article in your newspaper's editorial voice.

STEP 03 📋
Editor Reviews & Approves

Stories route automatically to the right editor by city and category. Review, refine, approve — all in one dashboard.

STEP 04 🚀
Published on Your City Page

Approved stories go live on a city-specific page — under your URL, your masthead, your brand.
